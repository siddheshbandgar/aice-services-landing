Meet the Founders

The visionary team building the future of enterprise AI. We're on a mission to transform how businesses operate with intelligent automation.

A serial entrepreneur and strategic mentor with over 25 years of experience building and scaling businesses globally. His expertise spans branding strategy, AI implementation, and business scaling, with a proven track record of identifying market gaps and transforming them into impactful tech-driven ventures.

An expert in Agentic AI and product engineering, specializing in building scalable AI solutions with a focus on measurable business impact. A VJTI alumnus with deep expertise in multilingual NLP, full-stack development, and building human-like AI agents with integrated safety layers.

Siddhesh Bandgar

A software engineer at Wells Fargo specializing in backend architecture, reliability, and scalable microservices. With over 3 years of experience building complex banking systems, he has deep expertise in Java, Spring Boot, REST APIs, and performance optimization. A VJTI Computer Engineering graduate and former Core Committee Member at Community of Coders, where he mentored students in software development and led technical workshops.
